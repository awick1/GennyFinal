Do It Yourself At-Home Restoration

Below, you'll find we have provided a number of tutorials and retailers to use to assist you in your at-home tractor restoration. At the bottom of the page, you will also find a contact form to send Kurt any questions you might have along your restoration journey.

General Steps

  • Step 1: Disassemble the Tractor

    • Take apart the entire tractor, inspect for defects, and order any necessary parts.

  • Step 2: Prep the Tractor

    • Clean any parts, if needed. Sand blast all parts and repair any dents or scratches.

  • Step 3: Make Mechanical Repairs

    • Disassemble the engine and discard all gaskets and bearings. Clean and degrease engine parts. Replace bearings and gaskets with new ones and reassemble the engine.

  • Step 4: Paint

    • First, coat each individual part with primer. Then, paint each part with 2 coats of paint.

  • Step 5:Reassemble the Tractor

    • Mount the engine to the tractor and reassemble all parts. Mount the wheels and apply new decals.

  • Run the tractor for quality inspection.
